# Kandt House Museum - Visitor Guide

## About the Museum

The Kandt House Museum (Maison Kandt) is located in Nyarugenge district, Kigali. It is the former residence of Dr. Richard Kandt, a German explorer who founded Kigali as Rwanda's capital city in 1907. The museum explores Rwanda's natural history, colonial history, and the evolution of Kigali as a capital city.

**Location:** Nyarugenge, Kigali (near the city center)
**Opening Hours:** Tuesday to Sunday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
**Closed:** Mondays
**Managed by:** Rwanda Cultural Heritage Academy (RCHA)

---

## HISTORY: DR. RICHARD KANDT AND KIGALI

### Who was Richard Kandt?

Dr. Richard Kandt (1867–1918) was a German physician, explorer, and colonial administrator who became the first Resident of Rwanda under German colonial rule. He is most famous for establishing Kigali as Rwanda's administrative capital in 1907.

### Founding of Kigali

When Kandt arrived in Rwanda, there was no permanent capital city. He selected the hill of Kigali as the site for the colonial administrative center due to its central location and strategic advantages. The city he founded has grown into a modern capital of nearly 1 million people today.

### The House Itself

Built in 1908, the Kandt House is one of the oldest surviving buildings in Kigali. Its colonial architecture represents a unique blend of European colonial style adapted for the Rwandan environment.

## WHAT TO SEE

### Natural History Collection

One of the finest natural history collections in Central Africa:
- **Flora specimens**: Hundreds of plant specimens collected from across Rwanda
- **Fauna collection**: Birds, insects, and mammals native to Rwanda
- **Geological displays**: Rocks and minerals from Rwanda's volcanic regions
- **The source of the Nile**: Kandt claimed to have found the most distant source of the Nile River in Rwanda (near Nyungwe Forest)

### Colonial History Gallery

Documents the German colonial period (1884–1916):
- Maps and documents from the colonial era
- Photographs of early Kigali
- Personal effects of Richard Kandt
- Colonial administrative records

### Kigali City Evolution

A fascinating visual history of Kigali's transformation:
- Historic photographs from 1907 showing a small colonial settlement
- Decade-by-decade growth of the city
- Architectural drawings and city plans
- Contemporary photographs showing Kigali's modern skyline

### Rwandan Natural Environment Gallery

Dedicated to Rwanda's remarkable biodiversity:
- Mountain gorilla conservation information
- Rwanda's national parks and protected areas
- The chain of volcanoes (Virunga)
- Lake Kivu and its unique ecosystem
