# Ethnographic Museum (Huye) - Visitor Guide

## About the Museum

The Ethnographic Museum is located in Huye District, Southern Province. It is one of Africa's finest ethnographic collections, depicting Rwandan history from ancient times to the modern era.

**Location:** Huye District, Southern Province, Rwanda
**Opening Hours:** Tuesday to Sunday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
**Closed:** Mondays

---

## WHAT TO SEE

The museum consists of seven galleries:
1. **Introduction Gallery:** Overview of Rwandan geography and history.
2. **Architecture and Traditional Housing:** Displays of traditional Rwandan dwellings.
3. **Agriculture and Animal Husbandry:** Tools and practices of Rwandan farming.
4. **Handicrafts:** Pottery, basketry, and weaving.
5. **Traditional Attire and Jewelry:** Royal and commoner clothing.
6. **Social Organization and History:** Clan structures and monarchical symbols.
7. **The Natural Environment:** Geological and ecological displays.

---

## COLLECTION HIGHLIGHTS

### Traditional Baskets (Agaseke)
Authentic Rwandan baskets used for storage and gifts, representing unity and friendship.

### Intore Dance Equipment
Traditional uniforms and instruments used by the famous Intore dancers.

### Royal Drums
Historically significant drums used in royal ceremonies and communication.

## MULTILINGUAL SUMMARY (INZU NDANGAMURAGE Y'IBY'UMUCO / MUSÉE ETHNOGRAPHIQUE)

### [RWANDA] Inyandiko mu Kinyarwanda (Inzu Ndangamurage y'Imyubakire n'Amateka i Huye)
Inzu Ndangamurage y'i Huye ni imwe mu nzu zikomeye muri Afurika zirimo ibikoresho n'amateka by'umuco. Iherereye mu Karere ka Huye, mu Ntara y'Amajyepfo, ku birometero 130 uvuye i Kigali.
- **Icyo wasura**: Ifite ibice birindwi birimo imyubakire ya gakondo, ubuhinzi n'ubworozi, amateka y'umuco, n'ubugeni bwa gakondo nko kuboha amaseke.
- **Ibikoresho**: Agaseke k'u Rwanda, ibikoresho by'ababyeyi, n'ingoma z'ingabe ni bimwe mu biranga iyi nzu.
- **Amasaha**: Ifunguye kuva kuwa Kabiri kugeza ku Cyumweru, saa tatu za mu gitondo kugeza saa kumi n'ebyiri z'umugoroba (9:00 AM - 6:00 PM). Kuwa Mbere irafunze.

### [FRENCH] Résumé en Français (Musée Ethnographique à Huye)
Le Musée Ethnographique de Huye est considéré comme l'un des meilleurs musées d'Afrique pour sa riche collection historique et culturelle. Il est situé dans le district de Huye, à environ 130 km de Kigali.
- **Expositions**: Le musée dispose de sept galeries couvrant l'architecture traditionnelle, l'artisanat (poterie, vannerie), les vêtements royaux et l'organisation sociale du pays.
- **Points forts**: On y trouve les célèbres paniers "Agaseke", les équipements des danseurs Intore et une collection exceptionnelle de tambours royaux.
- **Horaires**: Ouvert du mardi au dimanche, de 09h00 à 18h00. Fermé le lundi.
